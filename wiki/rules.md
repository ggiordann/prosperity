### Game Participation Rulesb

To ensure a smooth experience during Prosperity, here are a few important guidelines regarding team participation and solo play:

- Create an Accountb → You can register and join the game until the start of Round 1b. 

- Switch Teamsb → To  complete your signup process in Prosperity, you must either join a team via a pending invitation or start your own. Should you wish to change your team, you can do so until the end of Round 2b. After this, team formation is locked. 

## Code of Conduct on Discord

To ensure Prosperity remains a respectful and enjoyable experience for everyone, we expect all participants to follow proper conduct on Discord and in the game.

- Be respectfulb towards your fellow participants. Use appropriate language and avoid any form of harassment or discrimination.

- Be considerate of different experience levels.b For some, Prosperity is to learn new skills; others, to enhance existing skills; for the top few, to win.

- Avoid off-topic conversationsb to ensure the channels remain focused and helpful for everyone.

- Repeated violations will trigger warnings, temporary mutes, or bans. Failure to adhere to these rules may result in removalb from the Discord server and potentially the game.
